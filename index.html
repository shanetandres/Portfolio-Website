Tube TV Oscilloscope

Personal Project

Driving the deflection coils of a Cathode Ray Tube (CRT) with an audio amplifier to use as a rudimentary oscilloscope in X-Y mode. One of my first electronics projects, I gained experience using lab equipment, working with high voltage electronics, and soldering.

Tetrix

Group Project

An online co-op version of the classic game, with a twist - where every time a new player joins, the playing board gets wider. Working on the backend of the project, I learned about working with databases and implementing authentication using Firebase. Along the way, I gained experience using JavaScript.
